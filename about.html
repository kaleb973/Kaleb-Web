About Kaleb

I am an International Baccalaureate (IBDP) student and researcher with a strong focus on biomedical engineering.

My work centers on exploring novel scientific concepts at the intersection of synthetic biology and physics, with a specific interest in developing low-cost, non-invasive diagnostic hardware. Operating within a professional laboratory environment, I have built practical expertise in circuit design, sensor calibration, and microcontroller programming. I am an active competitor in state-level academic competitions, including the North Carolina Science & Engineering Fair (NCSEF), and a state-level finalist in the HOSA Biomedical Equipment Technician event.
